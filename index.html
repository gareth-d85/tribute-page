Tribute Page to China Mieville



China Mieville has been one of my favourite authors for a long time.
I was first introduced to his work by my dad, and I read Pardido Street Station first.
After that, I read King Rat then The Scar. One Day I hope they will make Perdido Street Station into a film.
Here is a list of his books that he has released.


Stand-alone novels

  • King Rat (1998)
  • Un Lun Dun (2007)
  • The City and the City (2009)
  • Kraken (2010)
  • Embassytown (2011)
  • Railsea (2012)

Bas-Lag series

  • Perdido Street Station (2000)
  • The Scar (2002)
  • Iron Council (2004)
